Privacy Policy

PromptPack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

1. Data Collection

PromptPack does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.

2. Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome's storage.local API to save your preferences (locked phrases, must-preserve facts, settings). This data never leaves your device.

3. Network Activity

PromptPack makes zero network requests. There is no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no cloud synchronization.

4. Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

  • storage — save your preferences locally
  • activeTab — read selected text when you trigger the extension
  • sidePanel — display the side panel UI

5. Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries.

6. Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a new effective date.

7. Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.

PromptPack is an open-source project. Not affiliated with OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google.
